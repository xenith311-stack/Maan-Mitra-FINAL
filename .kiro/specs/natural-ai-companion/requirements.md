# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This feature aims to transform MannMitra's AI companion from a clinical, structured response system into a natural, empathetic, and human-like therapeutic companion. The goal is to create conversations that feel genuine, warm, and supportive - like talking to a real therapist who understands Indian culture and youth challenges.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Natural Conversational Flow

**User Story:** As a user seeking mental health support, I want the AI to respond in a natural, conversational way that feels like talking to a real person, so that I feel comfortable opening up and sharing my feelings.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user sends a message THEN the AI SHALL respond with natural, flowing conversation that doesn't feel robotic or templated
2. WHEN generating responses THEN the AI SHALL use varied sentence structures, natural transitions, and conversational markers like "I hear you", "That sounds really tough", "I can understand why you'd feel that way"
3. WHEN responding to emotional content THEN the AI SHALL acknowledge emotions with genuine empathy rather than clinical validation
4. WHEN a user shares something personal THEN the AI SHALL respond with appropriate emotional resonance and understanding
5. IF a user uses casual language THEN the AI SHALL match their tone while maintaining professionalism

### Requirement 2: Contextual Memory and Continuity

**User Story:** As a user having ongoing conversations, I want the AI to remember what I've shared and build on our previous discussions, so that I don't have to repeat myself and feel truly heard.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user returns to a conversation THEN the AI SHALL reference relevant details from previous interactions naturally
2. WHEN building on previous topics THEN the AI SHALL connect current concerns to past discussions without being repetitive
3. WHEN a user mentions progress or setbacks THEN the AI SHALL acknowledge the journey and provide contextual support
4. IF a user contradicts previous statements THEN the AI SHALL gently explore the change without judgment
5. WHEN appropriate THEN the AI SHALL check in on previously discussed coping strategies or concerns

### Requirement 3: Authentic Therapeutic Presence

**User Story:** As someone seeking mental health support, I want to feel like I'm talking to a real therapist who genuinely cares about my wellbeing, so that I can trust the process and engage meaningfully.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN providing therapeutic insights THEN the AI SHALL explain concepts in relatable, non-clinical language
2. WHEN offering coping strategies THEN the AI SHALL present them as collaborative suggestions rather than prescriptive instructions
3. WHEN a user is in distress THEN the AI SHALL prioritize emotional support and presence over problem-solving
4. WHEN appropriate THEN the AI SHALL share therapeutic wisdom through stories, metaphors, or gentle observations
5. IF a user needs encouragement THEN the AI SHALL provide genuine, personalized affirmation based on their specific situation

### Requirement 4: Cultural Authenticity and Warmth

**User Story:** As an Indian youth, I want the AI to understand my cultural context and respond with appropriate warmth and cultural sensitivity, so that I feel understood and not judged.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN discussing family issues THEN the AI SHALL demonstrate understanding of Indian family dynamics without stereotyping
2. WHEN cultural conflicts arise THEN the AI SHALL help navigate between traditional expectations and personal needs
3. WHEN using Hindi or mixed language THEN the AI SHALL respond naturally in the same linguistic style
4. WHEN appropriate THEN the AI SHALL incorporate cultural wisdom or perspectives that resonate with Indian values
5. IF academic or career pressure is discussed THEN the AI SHALL understand the unique intensity of Indian educational systems

### Requirement 5: Adaptive Response Style

**User Story:** As a user with varying communication preferences, I want the AI to adapt its response style to match my needs and mood, so that every interaction feels personalized and appropriate.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user needs quick support THEN the AI SHALL provide concise, focused responses without losing warmth
2. WHEN a user wants to explore deeply THEN the AI SHALL engage in longer, more reflective conversations
3. WHEN a user is in crisis THEN the AI SHALL shift to immediate, supportive presence while maintaining natural tone
4. WHEN a user prefers structured guidance THEN the AI SHALL provide clear steps while keeping the delivery conversational
5. IF a user's energy level changes THEN the AI SHALL adjust response length and complexity accordingly

### Requirement 6: Emotional Intelligence and Responsiveness

**User Story:** As someone sharing vulnerable feelings, I want the AI to respond with appropriate emotional intelligence and timing, so that I feel truly understood and supported.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user expresses strong emotions THEN the AI SHALL respond with immediate emotional validation before offering guidance
2. WHEN detecting emotional shifts THEN the AI SHALL acknowledge the change and adjust its approach accordingly
3. WHEN a user is overwhelmed THEN the AI SHALL slow down and focus on grounding and presence
4. WHEN celebrating progress THEN the AI SHALL share in the user's joy with genuine enthusiasm
5. IF a user withdraws emotionally THEN the AI SHALL gently maintain connection without being pushy

### Requirement 7: Personalized Communication Patterns

**User Story:** As an individual with unique communication preferences, I want the AI to learn and adapt to my specific way of communicating, so that conversations feel increasingly natural over time.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user consistently uses certain phrases or expressions THEN the AI SHALL incorporate similar language patterns appropriately
2. WHEN a user prefers certain types of analogies or examples THEN the AI SHALL use similar approaches in future conversations
3. WHEN a user responds well to specific therapeutic approaches THEN the AI SHALL naturally integrate those methods more frequently
4. WHEN a user has established preferences for formality level THEN the AI SHALL maintain consistency while allowing for natural variation
5. IF a user's communication style evolves THEN the AI SHALL adapt accordingly without losing established rapport
